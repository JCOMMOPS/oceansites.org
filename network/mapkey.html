OceanSITES Map Key

Data collection codes

P - PHYS Physical oceanographic parameters are made at the site.
M - MET Meteorological measurements are made at the site.
G - GEO Geophysical measurements are made at the site.
B - CHEM Biogeochemical measurements are made at the site.
C - CO2 CO2 measurements are made at the site.
F - FLUX Flux measurements are made at the site.

PHYS measurements may include the following:
Current speed and direction
Water temperature
Salinity

MET measurements may include the following:
Precipitation
Wind speed and direction
Air and sea-surface temperature
Humidity
Barometric pressure
Solar and infrared radiation
Surface waves

GEO measurements may include the following:
Seismic movements
Magnetism

CHEM measurements may include the following:
Nutrients
Organic sediments
Dissolved inorganic carbon
Oxygen
Chlorophyll

CO2 measurements may include the following:
Carbon dioxide pressure in air and water

FLUX measurements may include the following:
Air and sea fresh water exchange
Air-sea heat exchange
Air-sea gas exchange
Volume of deep ocean currents

Data Type codes

The code is a number composed of separate tens and units digits.
Tens
0 private
1 public, RT (real-time)
2 public, DM (delayed mode)
3 public, bot RT and DM

Units:
0 NoAcc (not yet accessible)
1 Acc (accessible)
2 Acc soon (Accessible soon)

Thus, codes <10 are private.
11 is public, real time; accessible
20 is delayed mode. not accessible
