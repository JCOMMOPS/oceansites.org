Site information for the OceanSITES interactive map.

The sites are currently classified into two groups corresponding
to those use in the OceanSITES brochure:
1) those stations making multidisciplinary surface and subsurface observations;
2) moorings observing a limited set of surface parameters.

Stored queries are predefined selections for several cases of common site attributes.
surface - all type surface sites
subsurface - all subsurface type sites
station - all station type sites
PHYS - all sites making physical oceanographic measurements
MET - all sites making meteorological measurements
GEO - all sites making geophysical measurements
CHEM - all sites making biogeochemical measurements
CO2 - all sites making CO2 measurements
FLUX - all sites making flux measurements
